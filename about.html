About Sarthak

Started with with a individual journey to give back to socity now terned into a movement supported by a strong volenteers group, local govt and non-govt organizations.

Our Founder

Dr. Urmila Tomar

Dr. Urmila Tomar gave wings to Sarthak in 2012 as she established this NGO with an aim to increase the reach and effectiveness of multiple public welfare programs, led by the state and central governments.

Dr. Tomar has 40+ years of experience as an academician and philanthropist. Having served across various administrative posts in Madhya Pradesh's Department of Education. She is also contributing in various Government led initiatives like - Gandhinagar Eco-Sensitive Zone Monitoring Committee Member, District Ganga Conservation Committee (NMCG) & Ayog Mitra, Human Rights Commission India.

"Our actions for green initiaves and water consurvation is need of this hour and should be addressed by each and everyone" — Dr. Urmila Tomar, Founder & Director
