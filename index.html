Our Mission

A movement rooted in belief

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Jitender Aggarwal, Sarthak was born from a deeply personal experience. After losing his central vision to Macular Degeneration, he channelled his challenge into a mission — to build a world where disability is not a barrier to a meaningful life.

"My vision was diminishing, but I kept on believing — it is the challenges only that strengthen you." — Dr. Jitender Aggarwal, Founder
